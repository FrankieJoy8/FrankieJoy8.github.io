Staten Island


PAY A VISIT TO THE STATEN ISLAND 9/11 MEMORIAL

If you make the trip to Staten Island, a nice place to visit would be the 9/11 memorial. It is a beautiful spot, and you can spend some time remembering those who died.

TAKE A HIKE IN HIGH ROCK PARK

This park is a great spot for nature lovers. There are many hiking trails with beautiful scenery. It is a good way to get away from the sometimes-hectic city.

WATCH SOME BASEBALL AT RICHMOND COUNTY BALLPARK

The Yankees have their Single - A minor league team here. It is not the majors, but the games are still fun to attend. It is a great stadium, with a view of Manhattan, and you can see future Yankees before they make the Major League.
