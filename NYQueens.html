Queens


TAKE A TRIP TO CITI FIELD

Citi field is home to the Mets. It is a small, but nice stadium, - great for a family night at the ballpark. While they are not the hottest team in baseball, the Mets can still be fun to watch - and the tickets are cheeper than seeing at Yankee Stadium. If you are lucky, you might be able to catch a game in the Subway Series, and be able to see both New York teams play each other.

TAKE A TRIP TO THE QUEENS ZOO

The Queens Zoo is another small zoo. It is set up in a circle so you can see everything easily by just following the path. The petting zoo, in a separate part is relatively big, compared to other zoos in the city.

FLUSHING MEADOWS CORONA PARK

This is the site of the 1964 Worlds Fair. The Unisphere is there, surrounded by fountains. It is a beautiful park with a carousel, the Queens museum and the New York Hall of Science nearby.
