Brooklyn


HAVE SOME FUN AT CONEY ISLAND

Coney Island is worth the long subway ride. Bring some friends and spend the day riding each attraction. There are rides for all ages, but for those that are more adventurous, a must-do rollercoaster is the Cyclone. But if that is not for you, you could take a walk down the boardwalk. There are plenty of food options and access to the beach.

VISIT THE NEW YORK AQUARIUM

Aquariums are always great place to go with kids. Spend a couple hours roaming around with the fish. The aquarium is home to sharks, seals, rays, and more aquatic creatures. They even have sea otter feedings!

ENJOY NATURE IN THE BROOKLYN BOTANIC GARDEN

If the Botanical Garden in the Bronx was not enough - or you were not in that neighborhood - the Brooklyn Botanic Garden is another great place to be in nature and enjoy beautiful flowers.

STROLL THROUGH PROSPECT PARK

This is a great spot for a picnic, or just simply for walking around. The Prospect Park Zoo and the carousel are also located inside the park.

THE PROSPECT PARK ZOO

This zoo is relatively small, and would only take a couple hours, but it is a nice place that kids would love.
